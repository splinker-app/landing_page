01 How it works

Three taps to your
next regular crew.

No swiping, no group chats with 200 strangers. Splinker does the matching, you just show up.

01
🏃

Pick an event

Splinker auto-creates events across the city's most popular spots — each with a level, a pace, and a vibe. Find one that fits and tap to join.

02
🤝

Get matched

2 hours before the start, we sort everyone into small groups of 3–5 by level and personality. You'll meet 2–4 people who actually fit you — not the whole roster.

03
☕

Hit the chill zone

Meet at the spot and have fun together. Then head to the chill zone, where every group from the session comes together.

04
🔁

Rate & repeat

Rate the session and mark who you'd meet again. We'll match you with similar people next time.
